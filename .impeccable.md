# Design Context — Nexio Labs Website

## Target Audience
Prospective clients of Nexio Labs (a digital product / software agency). Decision-makers
evaluating the studio's craft and credibility — founders, product leads, marketing teams.
They judge the studio by the site itself, so the site IS the portfolio.

## Use Cases
Marketing / brand site. Four pages: Home (/), Services (/services), About (/about),
Contact (/contact). Jobs: understand what Nexio does, gauge quality of craft, build trust,
get in touch.

## Brand Personality / Tone
Editorial, magazine-grade, "The Digital Curator." Sophisticated, kinetic, award-winning,
confident. Inspired by Google Stitch "2026 Kinetic" + Awwwards-tier studios
(375.studio, zarcerog.com). Bold motion, asymmetric editorial layouts, sharp 0px corners.

## Locked Design Decisions (do NOT "fix" — intentional brand choices)
- **Palette:** midnight-blue surfaces (#080D1A → #212B4A) + orange primary (#F97316) +
  blue secondary (#2563EB). Dark-first by brand mandate. Keep it.
- **Type:** Newsreader (headline/serif), Outfit (body), Space Grotesk (label). Loaded via
  next/font CSS vars --font-headline / --font-body / --font-label.
- **Radius:** 0px everywhere. Sole exception: circular CTA button on Services page.
- **Motion stack:** GSAP + ScrollTrigger (scroll timelines) + Framer Motion (React) + Lenis
  (smooth scroll). Heavy, intentional, "goes overboard" by design.
- **Custom cursor + film grain + scroll progress** are signature elements — preserve them.

## Constraints
Next.js 16 App Router, React 19, Tailwind 3.4. Must stay accessible (respect
prefers-reduced-motion) and performant despite heavy animation. Real company data only.
